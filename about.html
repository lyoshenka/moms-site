I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker who has been practicing for 14+ years and specializes in improving the psychological health of individuals and families. My work focuses on individuals, couples, and children and their parents/caretakers.

My approach to therapy combines my characteristics as an individual – compassionate, charismatic, non-judgmental – with years of experience, an effective teaching style, and expertise in a variety of professional methodologies. I work with clients of all ages and provide significant relief and assistance. In my practice, I draw on years of education, my training under highly-qualified professionals, and my own practical experience in mental health clinics, private offices, and early intervention centers.

Currently, I work in my private practice in two locations – Brooklyn, NY and Wayne, NJ. I also do individual in-home consultations and treatment for children under the age of three and skills training for their parents using the DIR Floortime method. In addition, I teach DIR Floortime classes (on line and in person) and lead parenting skills groups and workshops.

I am fluent in English and Russian.

Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree in School of Social Work from New York University (NYU)
  • 3-year Post-graduate training at New York Institute for Psychotherapy Training in Infancy, Childhood and Adolescence (NYIPT)
  • Certification as a Training Leader & Expert DIR Floortime Provider from the Interdisciplinary Council on Development and Learning (ICDL)
