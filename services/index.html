In my practice I work with people of different ages, cultural backgrounds, goals and life stages.

I show small children how to learn self-control and express themselves in appropriate acceptable ways.

I help teenagers explore and establish their unique personality and path into the future as well as to relate to others with respect to self.

I counsel couples in improving their communication with each other and in their overcoming various life challenges.

I work with parents to improve the understanding of their child and to develop positive, supportive and creative ways in bringing up their great kids to the fullest of their ability.

I assist adults in going through different stages of their lives, adjusting to changes effectively, being able to find balance, motivation, positive energy and support.

I have knowledge and experience, care and compassion, a warm smile and an efficient teaching style.
