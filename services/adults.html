Adults

At different stages of our lives we find that we need somebody who can listen to what we have to say, pay attention to our needs, and make objective observations. Sometimes we encounter situations that make us feel uncomfortable or challenged, such as financial troubles or the death of a loved one. It is important to develop skills and strategies to be able to manage the situation and to cope with the emotional strain that it adds to our daily lives. Regaining a sense of control can be critical to stabilizing ourselves.

My approach

My approach focuses on providing supportive and safe environment for the person to feel able to share the problems and develop trust. I assist in developing motivation for change.
