My approach

Using similar relationship – based approach with respect for individual personality, family structure and cultural background, I assist the parents in developing deeper understanding of their child’s unique strengths and difficulties. I support and assist the development of parenting skills that empower parents and caretakers to strengthen their relationship with their child and support the child’s development.

Specialties

  • Engaging in the child-oriented play
  • Teaching parents supportive disciplining and assertiveness for the children
  • Long-term planning
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Setting effective limits
